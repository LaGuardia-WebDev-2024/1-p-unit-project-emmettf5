Jazz Music

By Emmett Fitzgerald

Jazz Music was a rebelious sound back then. Everybody was used to hearing the Bach and mozart inspired classical peices.
People heard it as something different, with a swing to the drums and the bass walking around.
It was a unique sound no one had heard of. This evolved to the jazz wave, where it was seen as sleek and cool, but hard to comprehend.
This led to people falling in love with the sound as it kept evolving through each generation, it led to hip hop, funk, rock, blues and everything has jazz influence hidden deep.


This live music relys on the listening of musicains and everybody magically hearing ideas out of an instrument.
